About me

Hassan Isah is the CoFounder/Chief Operating Officer (COO) of DigiSimplify Limited, a product design, reserach and development company located at Abuja, Nigeria. He is currently a fellow at TIIDELab where he is gaining more knowledge and further sharpening his coding and programming skills. He has also done and internship with ITSLE Fabrication Laboratory (Fab Lab) Abuja where he understudied as a junior python developer.

Hassan Isah graduated from the prestigeous Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, Kaduna State with a Bachelor of Science degree in Glass Technology and then he further proceeded to obtain a Masters degree in Environmental Resource Management at the Nasarawa State University Keffi, Nasarawa State but even as such the tech ecosystem has always been his calling.

He has worked with a handful of individuals and companies as well amongst which are as follows, Local Education Authority, Atmusive Innovation, Federal Character Commission, Maslaha Law Incorporation and lots more.

His goals and objectives as a software developer is to create dynamic and world class solutions to Nigerians, Africans and the world at large which is also the core mandate upon which he founded his company, DigiSimplify.

Aside creating technological solutions and solving problems, he enjoys watching football and playing it as well. He is a lover of good food and entertainment.
